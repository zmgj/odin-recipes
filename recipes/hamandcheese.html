Ham and Cheese Recipe

This and cheese recipe is really simple to make. You can use whatever cheese or cold cuts you like. Don't be afraid to mix different meats and cheeses.

Ingredients

  • ham
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Get two pieces of bread and butter one side each
  2. Toast the bread lightly
  3. Put desired cheese on each slice and toast again until cheese is melted
  4. Add ham on top of cheese
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
