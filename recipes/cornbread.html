Cornbead Recipe

This sweet and savory cornbread will make you fat. It is so delicious that it will finish within a day wanting you to make another one. This simple recipe uses only 12 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • cornmeal
  • flour
  • salt
  • sugar
  • butter
  • buttermilk
  • eggs

Steps

  1. mix all dry ingredients
  2. mix wet ingredients to dry ingredients
  3. pour in dish and put in oven for 30 minutes
